Delicious Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the crust by mixing flour, butter, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Roll out the dough and place it in a pie dish.
  4. Fill the crust with a mixture of sliced apples, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  5. Top with another layer of rolled-out dough and crimp the edges.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Let the pie cool before serving.
